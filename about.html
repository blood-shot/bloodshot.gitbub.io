IT'S YOUR FRIENDLY NEIGHBOURHOOD JASON BLOOD

This page showcases my graphic design work such as photography, food photography and typography. I am currently studying a Communication Design course at Federation University. My greatest passion in life is cooking, I have incorporated this love with my second love with is photography, and tried to capture true raw looking food. My goal is to find work in a food magazine one day. I have chosen graphic design as my pathway, because I am not really a talkative person...
